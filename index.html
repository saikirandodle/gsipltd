    About

    About Us

    Established in 1995, CV Praveen Contractor began its journey in the construction industry, specializing in residential projects. With a commitment to quality and client satisfaction, we have built a reputation for delivering excellence in every endeavor.In 2011, leveraging our expertise and experience, we founded Gowri Shanker Infra Pvt Ltd. This expansion marked our entry into handling industrial and institutional projects, further solidifying our presence in the construction sector.

    At Gowri Shanker Infra Pvt Ltd, we blend innovation with precision, catering to the diverse needs of our clients across various sectors. Our team of dedicated professionals ensures that each project meets the highest standards of quality and safety, adhering to strict timelines and budgetary constraints.With decades of experience and a proven track record, we continue to uphold our commitment to craftsmanship and integrity, striving to exceed expectations with every project we undertake.

    Turnkey Construction

    Labor Rate Construction

    we offer flexible and transparent labor rate construction services for building projects. This approach allows clients to directly control material costs while benefiting from our skilled labor force.
